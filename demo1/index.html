Hero.coli

Hero.coli is an adventure game which lets you explore a living world, to collect and combine functional DNA fragments in order to engineer and control the abilities of a bacterium and face obstacles and dangers.

Join the quest of a nano-robot and its bacterium companion Cellia to ensure the balance and prosperity of their living world.

Start your journey now.

Show us your mastery of synthetic biology by finishing the game in record time, and give us some ideas about how to make the game even better!

Make a Difference!

Help prevent drought and flooding

Geotag-X

Have you ever wished you could make a difference when you hear about earthquakes or flooding happening on the other side of the world?

At UNITAR/UNOSAT, we are developing Geotag-X—a platform for crowdsourcing the analysis and geotagging of media from disasters which lets you do exactly that.

By analysing photos and geo-referencing them, you improve the world's ability to respond to humanitarian crises.

At LATES, we're asking people to try their hand at helping out in two areas: Drought in Yemen and flooding in Yamuna, India.

Click on one of the challenges below, choose a project and off you go!

About us

Hands-on Citizen Science.

Hands-on Citizen Science

Have you ever wished you could make a difference when you hear about earthquakes or flooding happening on the other side of the world?

Do you want to create a new organism by engineering DNA?

Maybe do some hardware hacking with Iñupiat Whale Hunters in the Arctic Circle?

Then you could be a Citizen Scientist.

Citizen Science is science that anybody can get involved in - gathering field data, analysing and identifying data, playing problem-solving games, and 'hacking' together sensor devices to measure and impact your own direct environment. Even creating and running your own experiments.

Drop by our workshop on floor 3 and try your hand at being a scientist.
